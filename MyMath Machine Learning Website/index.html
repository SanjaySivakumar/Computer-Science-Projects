About Us

MyMath is a student-developed website that utilizes Machine Learning to create course schedules designed specifically based on the needs of each individual. After creating a free account and answering a few questions on the following form, a user will receive a schedule that allows them to work on specific sections of the below courses based on their daily and weekly time availability. Please note, however, that you will have to allot at least 2 hours per week towards MyMath for at least two weeks - we can't help you ace your Math test tomorrow if you start today!
